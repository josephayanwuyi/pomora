What is Pomora?

Pomora is a minimalist, distraction-free Pomodoro timer tailored specifically for students. Balancing demanding study schedules, deep focus, and necessary rest can be exhausting. Pomora helps you break your study sessions down into manageable intervals, training your brain to focus cleanly without burning out.

Why Pomora Was Built

Pomora wasn't created because I wanted to build just another productivity tool. It started from my own reading routine.

Like many students and lifelong learners, I know how exhausting long study sessions can be. Staying focused for hours often feels harder than the work itself. I began looking for a practical solution that could help me structure my reading sessions without adding more distractions.

That's when I discovered the Pomodoro Technique. The idea was simple but powerful.. work in focused intervals, take intentional breaks and repeat. I started applying this method to my own reading habits and quickly noticed the difference.

Pomora grew from that experience. It was built as a simple, distraction-free space that helps you focus on what matters most—starting the next session, staying consistent and making steady progress. No unnecessary complexity, no clutter, just a tool inspired by a real problem and designed to help others who face the same challenge.

How to use the Pomodoro Timer?

  1. Define Your Tasks: List out what assignments, study blocks, or readings you need to accomplish today.
  2. Set the Interval: Select your Pomodoro session (traditionally 25 minutes) and press START to begin focusing intensely.
  3. Protect Your Attention: Work diligently without checking social media, emails, or tabs until the timer goes off.
  4. Take a Short Break: When the alarm rings, step away from your screen for a 5-minute breather to recharge your cognitive battery.
  5. Iterate the Cycle: Repeat this process four times consecutively to complete a full study round.
  6. Enjoy a Long Rest: After four full Pomodoros, reward yourself with a long 15–30 minute break before diving back into your work.

Features Built for Students

Beyond just a simple countdown clock, Pomora features persistent task tracking, dynamic ambient visual states that shift color palette contexts smoothly between focus blocks and break periods, and minimalist audio feedback cues designed not to startle you out of your flow state.

Pomora also supports account-based synchronization, allowing users to securely save and access their tasks and schedules across multiple devices. Whether you switch between your phone, tablet, or laptop, your workspace stays consistent and up to date.

The goal is to remove friction, reduce distractions, and create an environment where deep focus feels natural and sustainable.
