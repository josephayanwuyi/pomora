What is Pomora?

Pomora is a minimalist, distraction-free Pomodoro timer tailored specifically for modern students. Balancing demanding study schedules, deep focus, and necessary rest can be exhausting. Pomora helps you break your study sessions down into manageable intervals, training your brain to focus cleanly without burning out.

Why Pomora Was Built

Most online timers are cluttered with ads, complex configurations, and noisy interfaces that defeat the purpose of "deep focus." Pomora was created to strip away the noise. By combining an intentional, warm aesthetic with essential task tracking, it creates a psychological safe space that gently encourages you to begin your next study sprint.

How to use the Pomodoro Timer?

  1. Define Your Tasks: List out what assignments, study blocks, or readings you need to accomplish today.
  2. Set the Interval: Select your Pomodoro session (traditionally 25 minutes) and press START to begin focusing intensely.
  3. Protect Your Attention: Work diligently without checking social media, emails, or tabs until the timer goes off.
  4. Take a Short Break: When the alarm rings, step away from your screen for a 5-minute breather to recharge your cognitive battery.
  5. Iterate the Cycle: Repeat this process four times consecutively to complete a full study round.
  6. Enjoy a Long Rest: After four full Pomodoros, reward yourself with a long 15–30 minute break before diving back into your work.

Features Built for Students

Beyond just a simple countdown clock, Pomora features persistent task tracking, dynamic ambient visual states that shift color palette contexts smoothly between focus blocks and break periods, and minimalist audio feedback cues designed not to startle you out of your flow state.
